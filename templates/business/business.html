ABOUT DOUGLAS

Douglas is a web marketer/ software engineer based in Tokyo.

After graduation from Gakushuin University in Mar. 2020, Douglas has been working as a web marketer at Mirai Kirei to learn skills that can help people achieve their goals in business.

From Jan. 2021, he started his career as a software engineer when he saw the possibility in himself of changing the job hunting environment for univ. students in Japan.

Since then, he has run the website "Kiite-me!", where univ. students can interact with their alumni asking about their jobs and industries.

WORKS

Kiite-me!

Kiite-me! is a yahoo-answers-like website that helps university students in Japan interact with their alumni. For example, the graduates can ask the alumni for tips for job interviews and what their jobs are like.
With the spread of covid -19, it has become harder for students to ask for advice from someone in person. I wanted to provide a solution to this problem.
